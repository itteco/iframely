Dissecting Tadagraph's Topics

Everything comes in a perfect order (though we might not always know that). We like our conversations and discussion to be organized and easily traceable as well. Therefore we have introduced [Topics] functionality in Tadagraph.

It's a vital part of our mark-up language that will help you curate the flow and stay organized. All you have to do is include topic name into square brackets to tag any message.

Watch this short video to learn everything you need to know about topics and how it helps in your everyday work and step on the path of becoming Tadagraph ninja.
